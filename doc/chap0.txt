  [1XAbstract[101X
  [33X[0;0YThe [5Xaaa[105X package is a [5XGAP[105X package containing methods for transducers that can
  be  represented  as  asynchronous  automata.  It  implements  the  processes
  described in the paper by Grigorchuk, Nekrashevich, and Sushchanskii [GNS00]
  for working with finite transducers and the rational group R.[133X
